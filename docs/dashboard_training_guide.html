How to use the Tree of Life dashboards

Eight self-serve tools, one app. Each takes an Excel export you already pull from SAP, applies the team's rules, and hands back a finished, download-ready workbook. This guide walks you through every one — what to upload, which buttons to press, and what comes out.

Before you start — the basics

Open the app

Go to the app link in your browser. Use the menu on the left to switch between the eight dashboards — the list matches the numbers in this guide.

Upload Excel

Every dashboard runs on the .xlsx exports you already download from SAP or the portal. Drag the file in, or click to browse.

Download the result

After processing, click the green Download button to save the finished workbook. Preview tables on screen first if you want to check it.

Nothing is stored

Files are processed in your session and cleared when you leave. Nothing is saved on a server, so re-upload each time you run a report.

The eight dashboards, one by one

1

Supplier Compliance Dashboard

Did suppliers upload their inbound documents?

Matches every SAP purchase order that has an inbound delivery against the documents suppliers uploaded to the portal for a given month. Suppliers with an inbound PO but no valid portal file are flagged as non-compliant and billed back automatically.

Files you upload

  • The SAP PO export (.xlsx — PO numbers, inbound delivery status, dates)
  • The Portal upload export (.xlsx — the supplier document list)

Steps

  1. Upload the SAP export on the left, the Portal export on the right.
  2. Pick the Report Year and Report Month.
  3. Click Generate Compliance Report.
  4. Review the on-screen Monthly Summary and compliance %.
  5. Click Download Excel report.

What you get

A multi-sheet workbook: Monthly Summary, matched vs. missing documents, pending TOL review, and a separate bill-back tab per non-compliant supplier with the charge owed.
Monthly SummaryMissing Portal FilePending TOL ReviewSupplier SummaryBB-{Supplier}
The month scope is a fixed audit rule: a PO counts for the month if any of its date columns falls in that month — there's no setting to change it. Excluded PO types (certain prefixes) are dropped automatically and reported on screen.
2

Delivery Fill Rate Dashboard

Where are we shorting outbound deliveries?

Reads a SAP/BW Delivery Fill Rate export and shows shortages and fill rates by plant, product, and product group, highlighting the biggest-dollar shortfalls so you know where to chase supply first.

Files you upload

  • The SAP/BW Delivery Fill Rate export (.xlsx or .xls)

The app finds the real header row and fills SAP merged cells for you.

Steps

  1. Open the sidebar and upload the export.
  2. Set the High Priority $ threshold (default $1,000) to flag big shortages.
  3. Optionally narrow with the Filters — plant, product, product group, date, status.
  4. Read the KPIs and charts; open the Download tab.
  5. Click Generate, then download the Excel report.

What you get

On-screen fill-rate KPIs, top-10 shortages, and plant/product breakdowns — plus a polished Excel report reflecting whatever filters you left active.

3

Sales Order Fill Rate Dashboard

How much demand is unconfirmed, and for whom?

The demand-side twin of the delivery dashboard. Reads a SAP/BW Sales Order Fill Rate export and surfaces unconfirmed demand and fill rates by key account, CDM, product, and plant.

Files you upload

  • The SAP/BW Sales Order Fill Rate export (.xlsx or .xls)

Steps

  1. Upload the export from the sidebar.
  2. Set the High Priority $ threshold (default $500) on unconfirmed demand.
  3. Filter by key account, plant, product, CDM, status, or priority; choose a sort order.
  4. Review KPIs and breakdowns on screen.
  5. Generate and download the Excel report.

What you get

Unconfirmed-demand KPIs, an unconfirmed-lines report, and account / CDM / product / plant summaries, exported to Excel.
SummaryUnconfirmed ReportBy AccountBy CDMTop 10
4

Daily Short Report Dashboard

Where does quantity fall out between order and invoice?

Takes the SAPUI5 Daily Short export and traces every case from ordered → confirmed → delivered → invoiced, showing the fill rate at each hand-off and the lines shorted along the way.

Files you upload

  • The SAPUI5 Daily Short export (.xlsx or .xls)

Steps

  1. Upload the export from the sidebar.
  2. Choose Top N per table (default Top 5) and any filters — plant, customer, vendor, order type, date.
  3. Read the Overview waterfall, then the Unconfirmed / Shorted-at-Delivery / Shorted-at-Invoicing tabs.
  4. Open the Download tab and click Generate Excel Report.

What you get

A workbook with a Summary of totals and fill rates, one sheet per short-analysis stage, and a Raw Data sheet with every line behind the numbers.
SummaryUnconfirmedShorted at DeliveryShorted at InvoicingRaw Data
The template's own summary/total rows at the bottom of the export are detected and excluded automatically — you don't need to trim the file first.
5

Overstock Report Dashboard

What stock is piling up near its last sellable date?

Matches on-hand inventory to the Last-Sell / BDM master and flags excess stock that is still sellable but approaching its last-sell-by date, split out by warehouse so each site sees its own list.

Files you upload

  • The Materials inventory export (.xlsx)
  • The Last-Sell / BDM Material Master (.xlsx)

Steps

  1. Upload the Materials file and the Master file.
  2. Set the date window — earliest shelf-life expiry to keep, and the last-sell-by cutoff.
  3. Click Process files.
  4. Preview each warehouse tab, then click Download.

What you get

A workbook with one sheet per warehouse, built to the business's overstock spec.

Donate / Dispose List Dashboard

What can no longer be sold?

The mirror image of the Overstock report. Uses the same two files to list stock at, near, or past its shelf-life expiry — product that should be donated or disposed of rather than sold — across every storage location, by warehouse.

Files you upload

  • The Materials inventory export (same file as Overstock)
  • The Last-Sell / BDM Material Master (same file as Overstock)

Steps

  1. Upload the Materials file and the Master file.
  2. Set the single cutoff: include shelf-life expiry on or before this date.
  3. Click Process files.
  4. Preview each warehouse tab, then click Download.

What you get

A donate/dispose workbook with one sheet per warehouse.
MississaugaCalgarySurrey
Overstock and Donate/Dispose are two halves of the same shelf-life picture: what's still sellable but overstocked vs. what's past selling. Same uploads, opposite side of the cutoff date.

Risky Inventory Dashboard

Strip the repeated rows out of the 180-day report

Automates the manual step you do today: the cumulative 180-day report repeats the first 90 days, so this removes those already-reported rows — comparing complete inventory lines, not just the Material number — and rebuilds the four-sheet workbook.

Files you upload

  • The 90-day report (.xlsx)
  • The 180-day cumulative report (.xlsx)

Steps

  1. Upload the 90-day report and the 180-day cumulative report.
  2. Click Process files.
  3. Check the count of rows removed and the cleaned preview.
  4. Click Download Risky Inventory Report.

What you get

A four-sheet workbook — the 90-day detail and summary, plus the de-duplicated 180-day detail and summary.
90D Detail90D Summary180D Detail180D Summary
8

Batch Quality Analysis

Catch bad batch numbers & expiry dates for review

Scans the SAP receiving history for likely batch-number and expiry-date data-quality problems, groups them into issues, and runs an AI reviewer over each group to explain the pattern and suggest what to verify. It's a review aid — the AI never edits SAP or invents corrected values.

Files you upload

  • The SAP receiving-history export (.xlsx)

Steps

  1. Upload the receiving export.
  2. Click Process Batch Quality File — detection and the AI review run together.
  3. Work through the tabs: Summary → Flagged Issues → Material Batch History → Review Results.
  4. Download the Excel review pack.

What you get

A flagged-issues workbook with each issue's risk level, the records involved, the AI's summary and recommended review steps, and questions to put to the supplier or receiver.
Upload & SummaryFlagged IssuesMaterial Batch HistoryReview Results
Treat the AI output as a starting point for human judgment, not a decision. It highlights patterns worth checking; a person confirms and fixes the data in SAP.

Rule of thumb: pick the dashboard from the left menu, upload the Excel it asks for, press the primary button, then download the workbook. If a file won't load, the tool prints a plain-English error — check you uploaded the right export, then try again.
